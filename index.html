Scott Kasper

About Me

Scott Kasper is a 32-year Emergency Medical Services veteran who has become a proven leader in EMS and Healthcare Administration. Using his strengths in strategic planning, operations, process improvement, revenue cycle management and more, Scott has successfully built and maintained high-performing systems that provide excellent clinical services with sustained positive financial results. Throughout Scott’s career he has successfully collaborated with State, County and local government officials, working diligently to effect passage of updated laws, and innovative regulations. He has taken his leadership from EMS and translated that into the hospital and other areas of healthcare, working collaboratively with physicians, nurses, and program administrators to improve care throughout the continuum.

Scott’s true passion is his family. He is a dedicate husband and the father of three amazing sons. When several of his family were diagnosed with Type 1 Diabetes, he became an active and outspoken advocate, educator and fundraiser for JDRF. He has served as a Board member, Chapter Board President, and is currently a member of the JDRF National Speaker’s Bureau. Scott is a USA Cycling certified Coach, and works on a national level with the JDRF Ride to Cure Program, combining his love of cycling with his passion to find a cure for Type 1 Diabetes.
